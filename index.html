The vision of the mentor men leadership forum

mentor men forum's vision is "RAISING GODLY, RESPONSIBLE, AND INTENTIONAL MEN". This assignment of the Mentor Men Leadership Forum(MMLF) is drawn from the scriptures, in the book of Titus 2 vs 6-8.

There the scriptures enumerated how men should conduct themselves. The vision of the Mentor Men Forum is therefore hinged on these scriptures. So we are sent to raise Godly, Responsible, and Intentional Men.

  • Being Godly: Is patterning a life after Christ! A life laced with the fear of the God of the Universe, good works,and integrity. A life that shows discipline, a life that can model and influence the next generation. It is also to be incorruptible and to showcase a lifestyle that cannot be condemned. To be sound in Faith!.
  • Being Responsible: Is being given to and taken by his duty that he may not be blamed. He must be trusted, reliable, and committed to complete a duty. Note that,'Good Success Is A Product Of Responsible People; And Responsibility Is The Price Of Greatness'.
  • Being Intentional: He is Deliberate about his lifestyle, and his relationships. He must be highly driven to achieve his goals, focused, and blind to distraction. His private life is as important as his public life. He is therefore intentional about the wellbeing of his home, loves his wife, and involved in the upbringing of his children. He is not into multiple sexual relations, i.e having a side-chick(a modern way of coating "immorality"). He is an honest man in his relationship with his wife. He is to be seen and perceived in his home, and to creat that effect around him. He is blind to any kind of distraction.
