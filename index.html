About mentor men leadership forum

Mentor men leadership forum is a faith-based platform dedicated to raising godly, responsible, and intentional men. We restore the dignity of manhood by mentoring men to embrace their God-given leadership role as husbands, fathers, and leaders in society.

Through mentorship, training, and accountability, we raise faithful men who build strong families, and a strong society.

The vision of the mentor men leadership forum

The vision of the mentor men leadership forum(MMLF) is raising godly, responsible, and intentional men." The vision is rooted in titus 2:", 6-8, which outlines how men are called to live and conduct themselves. Our mandate is to raise men who reflect these biblical standards in character, leadership, and responsibility.

WHAT IT MEANS TO BE:

  • Godly: Living a Christ-patterned life marked by the fear of God, integrity, discipline, sound in faith, and good works. A godly man lives a life that is above reproach, and models values that influence the next generation.
  • Responsible: Being dependable, trustworthy, and committed to duty. A responsible man takes ownership of his actions and obligations, understanding that greatness is built on responsibility.
  • Intentional: Living deliberately-about faith, relationships, family, and purpose. An intentional man is focused, discipline, faithful to his wife, actively involved in his children's lives, and guarded against distractions and moral compromise.

NB: To Be Godly makes you Responsible, and to be responsible makes you Intentional. The Mentored man while creating wealth, creates trust.
